  • Proper electrical connection and circuit check
  • Complete system commissioning and test run

Maintenance

Service & Maintenance

Regular servicing keeps your unit efficient, extends its life and prevents costly breakdowns — especially important in Trinidad's coastal humidity.

  • Filter cleaning and replacement
  • Indoor and outdoor coil wash
  • Drain pipe cleaning and flush
  • Refrigerant level check
  • Full electrical component inspection
Book a Service
Relocation

AC Relocation

Moving home or remodelling? We safely disconnect your existing unit and reinstall it at the new location — fully tested and ready to cool.

  • Safe disconnection and refrigerant recovery
  • Full reinstallation at new location
  • System recharge to manufacturer specification
  • Complete test run before handover
  • Tidy-up of old mounting site
Book Relocation
Repair

Gas Top-Up & Repair

  • Electrical fault diagnosis and repair

  • Compressor assessment
