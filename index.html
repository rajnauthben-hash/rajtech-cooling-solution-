What We Do

Expert Services
for Every Home

We specialise exclusively in split-unit air conditioning — so every job gets the focused attention and expertise it deserves.

AC Installation

Professional installation of split-unit air conditioners for homes and small businesses, set up correctly for maximum efficiency and performance.

  • Indoor and outdoor unit positioning
  • Secure mounting and professional setup
  • Drainage, line-set routing and sealing
  • Full test-run and handover check
AC Service & Maintenance

Regular cleaning, inspection and servicing to keep your air conditioner running efficiently, extend its lifespan, and avoid costly breakdowns.

  • Deep cleaning of indoor and outdoor units
  • Filter wash and evaporator coil clean
  • Performance and cooling capacity check
  • Honest advice on any concerns found
